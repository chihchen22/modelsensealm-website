Agent Team of NBA Greats

Mission. Models are tools, not oracles. They can be useful without being precise or accurate all the time. Model Sense is Chih Chen's solo practice connecting ALM modeling knowledge with practical use, teaching bank treasury and risk teams how models work, why they fail, and when to trust them.

How the team works. Chih briefs Phil. Phil scans the inbox, identifies intent, and routes work to the right specialist. Magic guards the knowledge base; the modeling team builds and validates; the book team writes and edits; Allen designs the public face; David and John keep the business clean. Specialists return work to the owner inbox for Chih's review.
